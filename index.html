Our Process

We understand that our partners are operating as small fish in the vast ocean of the world wide web. We know how frustrating it is to look for the right solution at the right price. Enter LSCubed. Here's how we operate:

  • Consult

    We provide an initial consultation, for free, to understand our partners' bottom line, what matters to them, discuss their short and long term goals, and explain how we can help. There's no commitment, until and unless, a client decides we're the right fit for their job.

  • Conceptualize

    Once we've got a good understanding of a partner's needs, we hit the drawing board to map out a way forward. We think this is an important, but often overlooked, step. Before we write thousands of lines of code that we think solves the problem, we offer the client a chance to review designs, concepts, and solutions we commit them to code. This helps us get it right, the first time.

  • Create

    Designs and concepts approved, it's time to put fingers to keyboard and make your web app a reality. We use the latest web development technologies, for web and mobile, but we don't let flashy get in the way of functional. We develop to look good, but, more importantly, to meet our partners' needs.

  • Commit

    With the finished product in hand, we go back to our partner for a demonstration. Depending on what type of solution we've customized, training might also be involved. Our partners view the product in a test environment to ensure strict adherence to the project goals. Once approved, we deploy the app to the internet for all to see.

  • Continue

    Not a necessary step for all partners, but we offer continued maintenance of all solutions we design, as often or as little as desired.
